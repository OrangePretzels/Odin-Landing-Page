There are 8 cycles of the moon called Lunar Phases. The cycle repeats once a month (every 29.5 days).

  • New Moon
  • Waxing Crescent
  • First Quarter
  • Waxing Gibbous
  • Full Moon
  • Waning Gibbous
  • Third Quarter
  • Waning Crescent
